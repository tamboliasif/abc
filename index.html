Frequently Asked Questions

Education is all about learning not studying. Today the focus of all institution is teaching and completing syllabus in a bound time. We believes in learning where students are trained to be acceptable.

  • What is refundable enrolment fee?

    We charge a nominal enrolment fee for our Full Stack Data Science and Full Stack Web Development offerings. This fee is entirely refundable once you complete the course and are selected for AlmaX. The refundable fee model helps us in filtering non-serious candidates and benefit the most eligible students.

  • Is it mandatory to opt for the placement drive?

    No, it is not mandatory to opt for the placement drive. However, the training program fee is refunded only when you opt for the placement drive.

  • What is the Industry Readiness program about?

    Industry Readiness Program (IRP) is a comprehensive program that aims to equip students with necessary skills, and mindset to excel in their career after graduation. Our program is designed to help you stand out in a competitive job market and become a valuable asset to any organisation that they will join.

  • What types of places hire people with a background in information technology?

    The types of places that hire people with a background in information technology include technology companies like cloud network providers and software services consultancies, banks and other financial institutions, and retail companies. Skilled workers in information technology are often in demand in many sectors, as there is still a great need for talent that understands the benefits that information technology can bring to an organization.

  • What are the best online IT courses?

    The best types of online IT courses for someone looking to work in this field will vary based on prior knowledge, experience, career path, and IT career goals. Individuals may consider pursuing different types of online IT courses — from short courses to full bachelor’s or master’s degrees — depending on how they support their growth.
